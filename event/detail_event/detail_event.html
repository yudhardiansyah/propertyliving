Indonesia Property Expo

If you are looking for a house, you must certainly visit event Indonesia Property Expo 2014. Since held on February 8, 2014 in Hall A & B Jakarta Convention Center, this exhibition was never empty of visitors invaded.


You can find no more than 500 housing projects, apartments, flats, shops, to warehousing. The property offered is not only located in Jadebotabek, but also in the region of West Java, Central Java, Yogyakarta, Java, Bali, East Kalimantan!


We offered affordable prices, start from USD 99 Million. Some promo KPR that you can get at this event include administration fee, KPR repayments until 25 Years, and assurance One Day Approval. Do you have planning to invest? Visit Indonesia Property Expo 2014 will be held until February 16, 2014.

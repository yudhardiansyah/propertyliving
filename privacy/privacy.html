Privacy

By continuing to use livingproperty.com’s services, you signify that you have read, understood and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy as amended from time to time in respect of livingiproperty.com’s collection, use and disclosure of your Customer Data.

The types of data that livingproperty.com collects depends on the circumstances of collection and on the nature of the service requested or transaction undertaken.


2. Purposes for Collection, Use and Disclosure

Generally, livingproperty.com collects Customer Data, either directly from you or from your authorised representatives (i.e. persons whom you have authorised, persons who have been validly identified as being you or your authorised representative pursuant to our then-current security procedures), from third parties (e.g., our service provider), or from publicly available sources, through our website, mobile services, and other channels including our roadshow counters in order to fulfil the following purposes, which include but are not limited to.


3. Transfer of information overseas

The livingproperty.com Head Office is based in Jakarta. Customer Data may be transmitted to data storage facilities where livingproperty.com keeps its central records or for backup purpose. Customer Data may be transferred to livingproperty.com’s offices and appointed agents in Singapore or other countries in connection with livingproperty.com performance of the contract with you.


4. Consent

Generally, in the course of making a purchase at livingproperty.com performing servicing functions such as processing your order such as signing up for an event or joining a contest, helping you with membership registration, transmitting promotion alert messages or accepting payment information, Customer Data will, by nature of the task or transaction, be provided by Customer to livingproperty.com. In such instances, consent will necessarily be implied from the Customer that they are agreeable to providing Customer Data in order for livingproperty.com to provide the requested or necessary service and/or product to them. However, where required by law, livingproperty.com will adopt an ‘opt-in' policy for obtaining customer consent, in which event, express written consent will be sought from you when collecting your Customer Data e.g., signing a form or checking a box. Where you make purchases on behalf of another person, you undertake and will ensure that the individual whose Customer Data is supplied to livingproperty.com has authorized the disclosure, is informed of and consents to the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy.


5. Access

livingproperty.com will, upon your written request to our Personal Data Protection Officer (see Section 10), allow you to access your stored Personal Data. Where permitted by law, livingproperty.com reserves the right to charge a reasonable administrative fee for this service. Where permitted by law, livingproperty.com reserves the right to deny you access to your Personal Data and may provide an explanation.


6. Retention

livingproperty.com will retain Customer Data for as long as it is necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, the legal or business purposes of livingproperty.com, or as required by relevant laws. When destroying Customer Data, we will take commercially reasonable and technically possible measures to make the personal information irrecoverable or irreproducible in accordance with the applicable laws.


7. Accuracy

livingproperty.com needs your assistance to ensure that your Personal Data is current, complete and accurate. As such, please inform livingproperty.com of changes to your Personal Data by contacting livingproperty.com


8. Security safeguards

livingproperty.com takes the security and protection of your Customer Data very seriously. As such, livingproperty.com makes reasonable security arrangements to protect your Customer Data against loss or theft as well as unauthorised access and undue disclosure.


9. Updates to the privacy policy

livingproperty.com will amend this Privacy Policy from time to time and the updated versions will be posted on livingproperty.com’s website and date stamped so that you are aware of when the Privacy Policy was last updated. Subject to applicable laws, the English version of this Privacy Policy will prevail over any version of this Privacy Policy in another language. In the event of any inconsistency in interpretation between the English version and any translation of the Privacy Policy, this Privacy Policy statement in English will prevail.
